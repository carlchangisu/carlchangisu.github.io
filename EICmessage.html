Visionary Editor’s Messages

Following are some of the messages I wrote while serving as the Editor-in-Chief for IEEE Software. I speculated future directions on SE research, such as the marriage between SE and bio-inspired research. Fortunately, many of my predictions became true in the next two decades.

Carl K. Chang, "Let's stop the bipolar drift" , IEEE Software, May 1990, p. 4.

Carl K. Chang, "Experimentalists who transfer technology", IEEE Software, Jan. 1993, pp. 6,9.

Carl K. Chang, "Is existing software engineering obsolete?"", IEEE Software, Sept. 1993, pp. 4-5.

Carl K. Chang, "Changing face of software engineering", IEEE Software, Jan. 1994, pp. 4-5.
